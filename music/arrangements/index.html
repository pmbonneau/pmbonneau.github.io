Musical arrangements

My favorite genre of music has always been video games OSTs (Original SoundTracks) and instrumental songs in general. Musical composition is a form of art, there is an infinite number of unique pieces that can be created. Composing a song that sounds good is not an easy task, it requires a lot of time, knowledge and the most important, experience.

My general experience with music composition is very limited, I'm kinda new in this. So, I need to start somewhere. Why not doing some basic piano arrangements of my favorite tracks first ? :^)
